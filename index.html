A new way of (not) seeing wind turbines


Unleashing the potential of wind

Wind energy is on the cutting edge of the green revolution, but still has some impediments due to its visual impact.
Our project is aimed at unlocking the whole potential of this renewable energy.

Promoting biodiversity

Biodiversity loss is one of the biggest challenges of our days: our project is intended at converting windmills in vertical hubs thriving with life

Artistic and historical heritage protection

Italy is well known for the cultural heritage it holds and the beauty of its territory.
Our projects wants to integrate windmills with it, in order to valorise the landscape and increase tourism
